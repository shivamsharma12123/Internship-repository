Technology: Data Science

1. What is Data Science?
Data Science is the process of collecting, analyzing, and interpreting data to solve problems and help businesses make better decisions.

2. Where is it used?
- Healthcare
- Banking
- E-commerce
- Education
- Finance
- Social Media

3. Required Skillset
- Python
- SQL
- Statistics
- Machine Learning
- Excel
- Power BI
- Communication Skills

4. Career Opportunities
- Data Analyst
- Data Scientist
- Machine Learning Engineer
- Business Analyst
- Data Engineer

5. Future Scope
Data Science is one of the fastest-growing fields. Companies are hiring data professionals worldwide, and demand is expected to increase in the coming years.
